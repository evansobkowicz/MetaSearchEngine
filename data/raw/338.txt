<p>"<b>We Belong Together</b>" is a song written, composed and performed by <a href="/wiki/Randy_Newman" title="Randy Newman">Randy Newman</a> for the 2010 movie <i><a href="/wiki/Toy_Story_3" title="Toy Story 3">Toy Story 3</a></i>. The song was nominated for several Best Original Song awards from various film society and movie awards committees. The song won <a href="/wiki/Academy_Award_for_Best_Original_Song" title="Academy Award for Best Original Song">Academy Award for Best Original Song</a> at the <a href="/wiki/83rd_Academy_Awards" title="83rd Academy Awards">83rd Academy Awards</a> in February 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Oscars_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Oscars-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>When he accepted his Oscar, which was presented by <a href="/wiki/Jennifer_Hudson" title="Jennifer Hudson">Jennifer Hudson</a>, his speech was a humorous one that <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Moviefone.com" title="Moviefone.com">Moviefone.com</a> says "stole the show". He made fun of advice he was given that "it's not really good television to take a list out of your pocket and thank a lot of people", saying that "I just have to thank these people. I don't want to. I want to be good television so badly."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>It was nominated for the <a href="/wiki/Broadcast_Film_Critics_Association_Award_for_Best_Song" title="Broadcast Film Critics Association Award for Best Song">Broadcast Film Critics Association Award for Best Song</a> at the <a href="/wiki/16th_Critics%27_Choice_Awards" title="16th Critics' Choice Awards">16th Annual BFCA Critics Choice Awards</a> in 2011 and Best Original Song at the 2010 <a href="/wiki/Denver_Film_Critics_Society" title="Denver Film Critics Society">Denver Film Critics Society</a>, losing both to "<a href="/wiki/If_I_Rise" title="If I Rise">If I Rise</a>" by <a href="/wiki/A._R._Rahman" title="A. R. Rahman">A. R. Rahman</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dido_(singer)" title="Dido (singer)">Dido</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-insidemovies.ew.com_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-insidemovies.ew.com-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DFCS2AW_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-DFCS2AW-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup> It lost Best Original Song awards to <a href="/wiki/Cher" title="Cher">Cher</a>'s "<a href="/wiki/You_Haven%27t_Seen_the_Last_of_Me" title="You Haven't Seen the Last of Me">You Haven't Seen the Last of Me</a>" at the <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_Film_Critics_Society_Awards_2010" title="Phoenix Film Critics Society Awards 2010">Phoenix Film Critics Society Awards 2010</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PFCNTKSBFo2_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-PFCNTKSBFo2-6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup> and "<a href="/wiki/I_See_the_Light" title="I See the Light">I See the Light</a>", from the film <i><a href="/wiki/Tangled" title="Tangled">Tangled</a></i>, at the <a href="/wiki/1st_Annual_Awards.com_Movie_Awards" title="1st Annual Awards.com Movie Awards">1st Annual Awards.com Movie Awards</a>.</p>
<p>At the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/53rd_Grammy_Awards" title="53rd Grammy Awards">53rd Grammy Awards</a> in February 2011, Newman was nominated in both the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grammy_Award_for_Best_Score_Soundtrack_Album_for_a_Motion_Picture,_Television_or_Other_Visual_Media" title="Grammy Award for Best Score Soundtrack Album for a Motion Picture, Television or Other Visual Media">Best Score Soundtrack Album for a Motion Picture, Television or Other Visual Media</a>, which he won with the <i>Toy Story 3</i> Soundtrack,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RNWGFTS3S_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-RNWGFTS3S-7"><span>[</span>7<span>]</span></a></sup> and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grammy_Award_for_Best_Song_Written_for_Motion_Picture,_Television_or_Other_Visual_Media" title="Grammy Award for Best Song Written for Motion Picture, Television or Other Visual Media">Best Song Written for Motion Picture, Television or Other Visual Media</a>. However, his best song nomination was for "<a href="/wiki/Down_in_New_Orleans_(song)" title="Down in New Orleans (song)">Down in New Orleans</a>" from <i><a href="/wiki/The_Princess_and_the_Frog" title="The Princess and the Frog">The Princess and the Frog</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8"><span>[</span>8<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>It is the first song on the <a href="/wiki/Film_score" title="Film score">film score</a> of <i>Toy Story 3</i>. The entire album was composed and conducted by <a href="/wiki/Randy_Newman" title="Randy Newman">Randy Newman</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RNWGFTS3S_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-RNWGFTS3S-7"><span>[</span>7<span>]</span></a></sup> Upon the film's release, Disney did not release the soundtrack album for <i>Toy Story 3</i> on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/CD" title="CD">compact disc</a>. It was only available as a <a href="/wiki/Music_download" title="Music download">music download</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lossy" title="Lossy">lossy</a> formats such as <a href="/wiki/MP3" title="MP3">MP3</a> and <a href="/wiki/Advanced_Audio_Coding" title="Advanced Audio Coding">AAC</a>, until January 2012, when <a href="/wiki/Walt_Disney_Records" title="Walt Disney Records">Walt Disney Records</a> and <a href="/wiki/Intrada_Records" title="Intrada Records">Intrada Records</a> released the soundtrack on CD.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9"><span>[</span>9<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
