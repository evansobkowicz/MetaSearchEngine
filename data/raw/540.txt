<p>The Inception Wiki is a place where you can freely view and edit material pertaining to Christopher Nolan's latest film, Inception! <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Inception_(2010_film)" title="Inception (2010 film)">Inception</a> is a sci-fi action-thriller written, produced, and directed by <a href="/wiki/Christopher_Nolan" title="Christopher Nolan">Christopher Nolan</a>. It stars <a href="/wiki/Leonardo_DiCaprio" title="Leonardo DiCaprio">Leonardo DiCaprio</a>, <a href="/wiki/Joseph_Gordon-Levitt" title="Joseph Gordon-Levitt">Joseph Gordon-Levitt</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ellen_Page" title="Ellen Page">Ellen Page</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ken_Watanabe" title="Ken Watanabe">Ken Watanabe</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Tom_Hardy" title="Tom Hardy">Tom Hardy</a>. It was made by <a href="/wiki/Warner_Bros." title="Warner Bros.">Warner Bros. Entertainment, Inc.</a> and was released on July 16th, 2010.
