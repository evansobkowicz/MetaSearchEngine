If the Warden is a bloodmage then it would be even more dangerous since he can mindcontrol.</p><p style="">The Archdemon is a powerfull dragon who can cause heavy spells and most importantly... he cannot be killed. If we remove those godly characters then its hard to say. Lord of the Rings have guys like Morgoth and Balrogs/Eagles/Dragons (if we go by that age) and Dragon Age have Demons/Dragons and spirts and a shitload of mages either mortal or immportal like Arcane Horrors.<br/><br/></p>
