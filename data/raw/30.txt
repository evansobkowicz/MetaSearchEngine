<p align="center"><font face="Comic Sans MS" size="6"><font color="#0000ff"><em><font size="7">The Little Prince<br/></font><font size="4">by: Antoine de Saint-Exupery<br/></font></em></font></font></p></td></tr></table></td></tr></table></td></tr></table></body></html>

<td>
<p align="center"><font face="Comic Sans MS" size="3">Journey to Asteroid B-612 and join the Little Prince on his adventure to find answers, friendship, and love.  Together, you and the Little Prince will encounter characters such as a king, a lamplighter, a geographer, a snake, a fox, and many others.  Each character provides a unique look into humanity, while providing lessons and morals.  Though the journey may become difficult at times, you will learn that sometimes in life, the journey or experience is just as important as the destination or lesson learned!<br><br>Enjoy! </br></br></font></p></td></tr></tbody></table></p><font color="#009966" face="Comic Sans MS" size="5"><font color="#0066ff">What to expect</font>:<br><font size="3">~During our studies of <em>The Little Prince</em>, you will face many obstacles on your journey.  Here is a list of the various obstacles that you can expect to encounter:<br/></font>       <font size="3">-Nightly reading homework<br>          -Nightly completion of pages in your <em>'Student's Guide to Asteroid B-612'<br/></em>          -Class discussions<br>          -In-Class activities focusing on symbolism and locating quotes<br>          -In-Class writing assignments<br>          -Possible reading check quizzes<br>          -An end of the book test<br>          -Vocabulary Review<br>          -Various other exciting activities!<br><br><em><font color="#006633">*Remember, you may use your 'Student's Guide to Asteroid B-612' on ALL of the above mentioned assignments!<br>
