</center></div><h1>Madonna pictures</h1>
<p>Madonna (born Madonna Louise Ciccone on August 16, 1958) is an American singer-songwriter, actress and entrepreneur. Throughout her career, many of her songs have hit number one on the record charts, including “Like a Virgin”, “Papa Don’t Preach”, “Like a Prayer”, “Vogue”, “Frozen”, “Music”, “Hung Up”, and “4 Minutes”. Madonna has sold more than 300 million records worldwide and is recognized as the world’s top-selling female recording artist of all time by the Guinness World Records. </p>
