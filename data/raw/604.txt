<div class="widget storyContent article widget-editable viziwyg-section-1024 inpage-widget-8939454 articleContent">
<span class="storyTop ">
<p>The controversies surrounding <a href="http://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/michael-jackson-hit-with-new-sex-abuse-claims-five-years-after-his-death-9650832.html">Michael Jackson</a>’s life following his sudden death in 2009 have been rich and various.</p>
</span>
</div>
<div class="widget storyContent article widget-editable viziwyg-section-1024 inpage-widget-6138699 articleContent">
<div class="body ">
<p>But few are as outrageous as the claims currently being made in a book based on the journals and private videos of the King of Pop’s ex-manager, Frank DiLeo.</p>
<p>
<span class="inline-image w460 leftAligned">
<img alt="DiLeo at the premiere of This Is It in 2009" height="628" src="http://www.independent.co.uk/incoming/article10034245.ece/alternates/w460/DiLeo-ThisIsIt-Getty.jpg" title="DiLeo at the premiere of This Is It in 2009" width="460"/>
<span class="inLineImageCaption">DiLeo at the premiere of This Is It in 2009</span>
</span>
DiLeo, who effortlessly took on the role of Tuddy Cicero in <a href="http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/films/news/martin-scorsese-in-shock-and-sorrow-after-death-on-set-of-new-film-silence-10011131.html">Martin Scorsese</a>’s classic mob movie <em>Goodfellas</em>, was in charge of Jackson's career at the height of his fame in the Eighties.</p>
<p>He died in 2011 at 63 following the complications of heart surgery. 
<object class="BrightcoveExperience" id="myExperience 3898971147001">
<param name="bgcolor" value="#FFFFFF">
<param name="width" value="460">
<param name="height" value="301">
<param name="playerID" value="753144093001">
<param name="playerKey" value="AQ~~,AAAAkVf4sTE~,ZHihQoc0Mak3KW61gTbGinrWzI69us3-">
<param name="isVid" value="true">
<param name="isUI" value="true">
<param name="dynamicStreaming" value="true">
<param name="@videoPlayer" value="3898971147001">
</param></param></param></param></param></param></param></param></param></object>
According to the <em><a href="http://pagesix.com/2015/02/07/jacko-underwent-plastic-surgery-to-not-look-like-abusive-dad/" target="_blank">New York Post</a></em>, DiLeo behind a number of personal accounts and footage, during which he talked about Jackson. The documents have since been compiled into the post-humus biography<em> DiLeo: I Am Going To Set The Record Straight </em>by his former business partner Mark Lamica.</p>
<p><iframe frameborder="0" height="350" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/qo5jJpHtI1Y" width="460"></iframe> </p>
<p>In it, Lamica writes that DiLeo shared his knowledge of a planned Mafia contract to kill Michael Jackson – and how he managed to stop it from happening.</p>
<p>DiLeo also spoke extensively about Jackson’s changing appearance.</p>
<p>Lamica said: “Michael told Frank in a phone call that he couldn't bear to see his father’s [Joseph Jackson] face when he looked in the mirror.</p>
<p>
<div class="esi-gallery" data-galleryid="9233133" id="gallery-9233133"><a href="http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/music/features/michael-jackson-from-child-star-to-king-of-pop-9233133.html">Michael Jackson: From child star to king of pop</a></div>
 </p>
<p>“Frank had told him he needed to stop changing his face, because he was becoming unrecognisable to his fans.”</p>
<p>
<span class="inline-image w460 leftAligned">
<img alt="" height="345" src="http://www.independent.co.uk/incoming/article9661549.ece/alternates/w460/Michael-Jackson-8.jpg" title="Jackson at a press conference in Paris in 1996" width="460"/>
<span class="inLineImageCaption">Jackson at a press conference in Paris in 1996</span>
</span>
Lamica also claims the book will include details about his Neverland Ranch deal with Colony Capital and his record label, as well as “naming and shaming” those in the industry DiLeo felt had exploited him.</p>
<p>The release date for the book is yet to be confirmed.</p>
<p>A spokesperson for Michael Jackson’s estate is yet to comment on the claims.</p>
