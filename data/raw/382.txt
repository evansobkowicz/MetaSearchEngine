Just one problem: my insiders say that deal (which used to be based at Warner Bros) was set and signed back with <a data-tag="disney" href="http://deadline.com/tag/disney/" id="auto-tag_disney">Disney</a> in March, not recently, and just never announced. What <span style="text-decoration: underline;">is</span> new is that Depp is in final discussions at Disney for the much anticipated <a href="http://www.deadline.com/tag/alice-in-wonderland/"><em><a data-tag="alice-in-wonderland" href="http://deadline.com/tag/alice-in-wonderland/" id="auto-tag_alice-in-wonderland">Alice In Wonderland</a></em></a> sequel <a href="http://www.deadline.com/2013/05/james-bobbin-alice-in-wonderland-sequel-director-in-talks/">with James Bobin (of the recent <em>Muppets</em> reboots) subbing for Tim Burton as director</a>. Johnny’s turn as the Mad Hatter in that 3D modern classic, of course, made over $1B worldwide grosses for the studio in 2010. Old news is that Depp will reprise Capt Jack Sparrow in Disney’s <em>Pirates Of The Caribbean 5</em> dated for Summer 2015 and directed by <em>Kon-Tiki</em> filmmakers Joachim Rønning and Espen Sandberg.</p>
