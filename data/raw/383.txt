<p>A 19 year old girl, Alice, is betrothed to an English nobleman. Alice runs away at her engagement party where she falls in a hole and ends up in a surreal place called Underland. Alice understands her reason for coming to Underland, to conquer the monster Jabberwocky and the Red Queen and restore peace to her throne.</p>

Writers-Linda Woolverton, Lewis Carroll<br/>
Director-Tim Burton</p>

Dimensions-18″x24″</p>
