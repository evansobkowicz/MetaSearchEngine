<div id="content-body-inner">
<div class="node nodetype-page" id="node-145566">

<p><b>Join the new TeamCeline, Celine’s official online VIP Club.</b></p>
<p>Create a free account on celinedion.com and automatically become a member of TeamCeline, the online VIP Club for Celine fans. A membership offers you many benefits: </p>
<ul>
<li>Exclusive Ticket Offers</li>
<li>Discounts on all merchandise at the online Boutique; quickly build or add to your Celine Dion collection</li>
<li>Exclusive content and promotions available only for TeamCeline members; discover what VIP truly means</li>
<li>A profile that you can personalize and a member blog; make TeamCeline your own</li>
<li>The ability to comment on news, photos, videos and songs; you will see how interactive the site can be</li>
<li>The ability to upload your own photos; leave your mark on <a href="http://www.celinedion.com" title="www.celinedion.com">www.celinedion.com</a></li>
<li>Access to Forums, share your passion and discuss all things Celine with other community members</li>
<li>Plus more!</li>
</ul>
<p>Finally, the icing on the cake, you can link your TeamCeline account with Facebook and/or Twitter and share your activities within the VIP Club with all your friends.</p>
