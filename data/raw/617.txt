<p><b>Michael Joseph Jackson, Jr.</b> (born February 13, 1997), also known as <b>Prince Michael Jackson I</b>, is an American television personality and actor. He is the eldest child of pop singer/songwriter <a href="/wiki/Michael_Jackson" title="Michael Jackson">Michael Jackson</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_career">Early life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Prince_Michael_Jackson_I&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Michael_Jackson3_2006_(cropped_version).jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="495" data-file-width="597" height="182" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a5/Michael_Jackson3_2006_%28cropped_version%29.jpg/220px-Michael_Jackson3_2006_%28cropped_version%29.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a5/Michael_Jackson3_2006_%28cropped_version%29.jpg/330px-Michael_Jackson3_2006_%28cropped_version%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a5/Michael_Jackson3_2006_%28cropped_version%29.jpg/440px-Michael_Jackson3_2006_%28cropped_version%29.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a>

<p>Prince Michael Jackson I, commonly known as Prince, is the eldest son of <a href="/wiki/Michael_Jackson" title="Michael Jackson">Michael Jackson</a> and <a href="/wiki/Debbie_Rowe" title="Debbie Rowe">Debbie Rowe</a>. Jackson was named after both his paternal great-grandfather and great-great grandfather, who were also called Prince. He has one sister, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Paris-Michael_Katherine_Jackson" title="Paris-Michael Katherine Jackson">Paris-Michael Katherine Jackson</a> and one half-brother <a href="/wiki/Prince_Michael_Jackson_II" title="Prince Michael Jackson II">Prince Michael Jackson II</a> (also known as "Blanket"). Prince Michael Jackson I was born on February 13, 1997, at <a href="/wiki/Cedars-Sinai_Medical_Center" title="Cedars-Sinai Medical Center">Cedars-Sinai Medical Center</a> in Los Angeles, California.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Michael_Jackson_and_wife_become_parents_of_baby_boy_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Michael_Jackson_and_wife_become_parents_of_baby_boy-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> Prince Michael was raised by his father, who received full custody rights following his divorce from Rowe in 1999. Rowe had stated that it was her intention that Michael would raise and have custody of the children.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Jackson.27s_mother_granted_custody_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Jackson.27s_mother_granted_custody-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> Prince spent the early part of his childhood at <a href="/wiki/Neverland_Ranch" title="Neverland Ranch">Neverland Ranch</a> with his siblings. His father made longtime friends and actors <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Taylor" title="Elizabeth Taylor">Elizabeth Taylor</a> and <a href="/wiki/Macaulay_Culkin" title="Macaulay Culkin">Macaulay Culkin</a> the godparents of Jackson and his sister Paris.</p>
<p>In 2013, Prince Michael became a correspondent for <i><a href="/wiki/Entertainment_Tonight" title="Entertainment Tonight">Entertainment Tonight</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup> He made his acting debut in the television series <i><a href="/wiki/90210_(TV_series)" title="90210 (TV series)">90210</a></i> in March 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup> Prince has expressed a desire to do more acting in the future and he is also interested in film making.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Death_of_Michael_Jackson">Death of Michael Jackson</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Prince_Michael_Jackson_I&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Death of Michael Jackson">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On June 25, 2009 <a href="/wiki/Michael_Jackson" title="Michael Jackson">Michael Jackson</a> died from <a href="/wiki/Cardiac_arrest" title="Cardiac arrest">cardiac arrest</a> in his rented <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Holmby_Hills" title="Holmby Hills">Holmby Hills</a> mansion due to an overdose of the anesthetic <a href="/wiki/Propofol" title="Propofol">Propofol</a>. Following his father's death in 2009, Prince's grandmother <a href="/wiki/Katherine_Jackson" title="Katherine Jackson">Katherine</a> was made the permanent guardian of him, Paris and their half-brother Prince Michael II, while Rowe obtained visitation rights.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Jackson.27s_mother_granted_custody_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Jackson.27s_mother_granted_custody-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> Prince attended his fathers public memorial at the <a href="/wiki/Staples_Center" title="Staples Center">Staples Center</a> on July 7, 2009. He came onstage with family and other performers to sing his father's songs, <a href="/wiki/We_Are_the_World" title="We Are the World">We Are the World</a> and <a href="/wiki/Heal_the_World" title="Heal the World">Heal the World</a>. He stayed onstage with members of his family while some gave eulogies. Prince was asked if he wanted to speak at the memorial but he declined. In 2010 Prince, along with sister Paris, came on stage at the <a href="/wiki/52nd_Annual_Grammy_Awards" title="52nd Annual Grammy Awards">52nd Annual Grammy Awards</a> to accept a lifetime achievement award on behalf of their father. In 2012, Katherine Jackson split custody of Prince and his siblings with his older cousin T.J. Jackson, who is the son of Prince's uncle <a href="/wiki/Tito_Jackson" title="Tito Jackson">Tito Jackson</a> and a member of the group <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/3T_(group)" title="3T (group)">3T</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Prince_Michael_Jackson_I&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Prince and his sister Paris started attending <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Buckley_School_(California)" title="The Buckley School (California)">The Buckley School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sherman_Oaks,_Los_Angeles" title="Sherman Oaks, Los Angeles">Sherman Oaks</a> shortly after their father's death.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>On June 26, 2013 Prince testified in court over the wrongful death lawsuit against AEG live. The lawsuit alleges AEG was negligent in hiring physician Dr Conrad Murray, the doctor who served two years for the death of <a href="/wiki/Michael_Jackson" title="Michael Jackson">Michael Jackson</a>.</p>
<p>Jackson, his grandmother Katherine and his two siblings are in the works of putting together a documentary film titled <i>Remembering Michael</i> in remembrance of his deceased father via campaigning fans to donate funds to the site <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/FundAnything" title="FundAnything">FundAnything</a> to pay for costs for the making of the film.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7"><span>[</span>7<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
