<div data-action="Contextual" data-category="Article" data-track-tags="a" id="article-body">
<p><span>What a week for surprise weddings!</span></p>
<p><span>Riley Keough and Ben Smith-Petersen tied the knot on Wednesday night in an intimate ceremony in Napa, California, according to multiple reports. </span><span>The newlyweds exchanged vows in front of celebrity friends including Kristen Stewart, Cara Delevingne, Zoë Kravitz, Abby Lee, and Courtney Eaton according to E! Online. Stewart's attendance is notable, but more on that below.<br/></span></p>
<p><span>Keough, </span><span>the 25-year-old granddaughter of Elvis Presley, met the Australian stuntman on the set of <em>Mad Max: Fury Road</em>. They were first spotted out together in January 2014 and announced their <a href="https://celebrity.yahoo.com/blogs/celeb-news/rock--n--roll-royalty-riley-keough-engaged-193540758.html" target="_blank">engagement</a> in August.</span></p>
<p><span>"So that happened," Smith-Petersen captioned an image of the couple is lying in bed, with Riley flashing her new bling.</span></p>
<p><span>A few months later Keough celebrated her bachelorette party at the Madonna Inn in San Luis Obisbo, California. Her BFF Kristen Stewart was also in attendance.</span></p>
<p><span>Before Smith-Petersen, Keough was consistently (and falsely) rumored to be dating Robert Pattinson in 2013. <br/></span></p>
<p><span>"I don't want to say who she's with or not; I will say who she's not with and that is a complete lie, and the silliest thing I've ever heard, is that she was with Robert Pattinson because her and Kristen Stewart are like best friends," Lisa Marie Presley <a href="http://www.eonline.com/news/471750/lisa-marie-presley-says-daughter-riley-keough-never-dated-robert-pattinson-calls-rumors-a-complete-lie" target="_blank">told</a> Andy Cohen on <em>Watch What Happens Live</em>. "So that was the craziest rumor that I will put to bed."</span></p>
<p><span>This is the first marriage for Riley, who was engaged to her <em>Magic Mike</em> co-star Alex Pettyfer in 2012.</span></p>
<p><span>Look for Keough and Smith-Petersen to make their red carpet debut soon as <em>Mad Max: Fury Road</em> hits theaters on May 15.</span></p></div><!-- #article-body -->
