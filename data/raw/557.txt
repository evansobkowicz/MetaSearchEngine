<p><img alt="" src="/contributor/upload/125858/images/1402636363(1).jpg" style="height:428px;width:500px"/></p>
<p> </p>
<p>
Welcome to the new Coast to Coast AM radio show. George Knapp hosts coming directly to you from Las Vegas, NV. Topics of interest in this broadcast are as follows, Honey Booboo’s mom and dad splits up, Kardashians, George explores the up and downs of celebrityhood, Investigative reporter Maggie Jessup interviewed, ” Fame is the most powerful force in our society”, Fame run amok, Beatlemaina, Fame, celebrity, are they all what there cracked up to be?</p>
<p>
 <br/>
 <br/>
Coast to Coast AM airs on more than 560 stations in the U.S., as well as Canada, Mexico and Guam, and is heard by nearly three million weekly listeners. With hosts George Noory, George Knapp (weekend), and guest weekend hosts, it is the most listened to overnight radio program in North America.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>
 <br/>
A media phenomenon, Coast to Coast AM deals with UFOs, strange occurrences, life after death, and other unexplained (and often inexplicable) phenomena. Coast to Coast AM is overnight talk radio with daytime ratings.</p>
