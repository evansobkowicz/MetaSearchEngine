<h1>Australian Bee Gees show comes to Cleveland's PlayhouseSquare</h1>
<p class="article-time">
<span></span>
<span>Published: </span><time datetime="2015-02-09T00:00:00-05:00">February 9, 2015 12:00AM</time>
</p>
<div class="content"><p>The Australian Bee Gees Show, a celebration and multi-media theatrical interpretation of one of the most influential and famous musical groups of all time makes its debut appearance in Cleveland Feb.18 at Playhouse Square's State Theatre.</p>
<p>Having performed in more than 40 countries, the show was courted by Las Vegas where it premiered in February 2011 as a resident show to the tune of a multi-year contract.</p>
<p>The Bee Gees record-breaking music is captured on stage with mesmerizing accuracy in sound and mannerisms as it travels on a nostalgic trip through the legacy of the Bee Gees while celebrating more than four decades of the infectious music written by the Gibb Brothers.</p>
