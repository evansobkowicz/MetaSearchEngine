<h1 class="post-title entry-title">
			Audition Notice: And Then There Were None			        </h1>
<div class="post-meta clearfix">
<span class="printonly">Categories: </span>
<p class="meta_categories"><a href="http://www.onstageuxbridge.com/category/2014season/" rel="category tag">2014/15 Season</a></p>
<span class="updated"><span class="value-title" title="2014-11-10T21:05"></span></span>
</div>
<div class="entry-content clearfix">
<p><img alt="" class="alignleft" height="387" src="http://events.cardiff.ac.uk/files/2014/04/and-then-there-were-none1.jpg" width="274"/>OnStage Uxbridge announces auditions for the classic Agatha Christie mystery</p>
<p><strong>AND THEN THERE WERE NONE</strong><br/>
<strong>Directed by Samantha Smilovic</strong><br/>
<strong> Produced by Conrad Boyce</strong></p>
<p><strong>Show Dates</strong><br/>
Apr. 2-11, 2015, Uxbridge Music Hall</p>
<p><strong>Auditions</strong><br/>
Sun., Dec. 7 at 2 p.m. and Tues., Dec. 9 at 7 p.m.<br/>
Sandford Community Hall, 433 Sandford Road</p>
<p><strong>Callbacks</strong><br/>
Thurs., Dec. 11 8 p.m. Same Locale</p>
<p>No preparation necessary, cold readings from the script. Just bring your best British accent!</p>
<p>If you’d like a copy of the script to peruse in advance, please contact Conrad at<strong><a shape="rect"> conradboyce@powergate.ca</a></strong></p>
<p>If you’d like more information on the cast requirements, please contact Samantha at <strong><a shape="rect">samstag@hotmail.com.</a></strong></p>
<p>Here are the characters we need you to play (8 males, 3 females):</p>
<p>Vera: late teens – early thirties. Intelligent, resourceful, independent. A secretary.<br/>
Lombard: late twenties – early forties. Confident, strong, a bit of a scoundrel. An adventurer.<br/>
Wargrave: late fifties on. Focused, motivated, mindful. A judge.<br/>
Blore: late thirties – late fifties. Suspicious, foolhardy, certain in his convictions. An investigator.<br/>
Armstrong: late thirties – late forties. Nervous, gullible, cowed. A doctor.<br/>
Emily: mid-forties on. Rigid, cold, religious. A spinster.<br/>
MacKenzie: fifties on. Worn down, fatigued, confused. A retired general.<br/>
Marston: late teens – late twenties. Reckless, immature, self-involved. Rich.<br/>
Rogers: early thirties – late fifties. Misanthropic, domineering, self – important. A butler.<br/>
Ethel: early thirties – late fifties. Haunted, hysterical, heart in her throat. A cook.<br/>
Naracott: early thirties – late fifties. Proponent of serving class unrest, cockney. A boatman.</p>
<p>Look forward to seeing you at auditions!</p>
