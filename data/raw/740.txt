<p><b>Celine</b> is the second <a href="/wiki/Residency_show" title="Residency show">residency show</a> by Canadian recording artist <a href="/wiki/Celine_Dion" title="Celine Dion">Celine Dion</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-press_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-press-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> The show is performed at <a href="/wiki/The_Colosseum_at_Caesars_Palace" title="The Colosseum at Caesars Palace">The Colosseum at Caesars Palace</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Las_Vegas,_Nevada" title="Las Vegas, Nevada">Las Vegas, Nevada</a> beginning 15 March 2011, with an estimated 70 performances per year.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> The show ranked 26th in Pollstar's "Top 50 Worldwide Tour (Mid-Year)", earning over 20 million dollars.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup> Being seen by over 200,000 people, the show became the number one show in 2011 (for North America). The show has also made Dion the "most profitable music act in Las Vegas" since <a href="/wiki/Elvis_Presley" title="Elvis Presley">Elvis Presley</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>According to <a href="/wiki/Pollstar" title="Pollstar">Pollstar</a> end of year data (2011 - 2013), the show has grossed $116.5 million, playing to 726,346.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pollstarpro.com_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-pollstarpro.com-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ReferenceA_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-ReferenceA-7"><span>[</span>7<span>]</span></a></sup> In March 2013, <a href="/wiki/Ren%C3%A9_Ang%C3%A9lil" title="René Angélil">René Angélil</a> announced that Dion has extended her residency contract to 2019. However, it appears only a 2015-2017 extension was finalized.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8"><span>[</span>8<span>]</span></a></sup> Due to a statement made by Celine and <a href="/wiki/Caesars_Palace" title="Caesars Palace">Caesars</a> on 13 August 2014, all planned shows through 22 March 2015 have been cancelled.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9"><span>[</span>9<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Lesley_Messer_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-Lesley_Messer-10"><span>[</span>10<span>]</span></a></sup> On 29 October 2014, the Las Vegas Sun reported that Celine is "getting ready to come back to work".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11"><span>[</span>11<span>]</span></a></sup> Another article was published in January 2015 on <i><a href="/wiki/TMZ" title="TMZ">TMZ</a></i> revealing that Dion is most likely not coming back to perform until the end of the year.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12"><span>[</span>12<span>]</span></a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Celine_(residency_show)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<tr>
<td style="width: 20px; vertical-align: top; border: none; color: #B2B7F2; font-size: 40px; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-weight: bold; line-height: .6em; text-align: left; padding: 10px 10px;">“</td>

It's going to be a very beautiful show, and I think we'll be raising the bar higher than we've ever done before. <td style="width: 20px; vertical-align: bottom; border: none; color: #B2B7F2; font-size: 40px; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-weight: bold; line-height: .6em; text-align: right; padding: 10px 10px;">”</td>

<p>On 15 March 2011, Dion began a three year residency at <a href="/wiki/The_Colosseum_at_Caesars_Palace" title="The Colosseum at Caesars Palace">The Colosseum at Caesars Palace</a>. The new show features 31 musicians, consisting of a full orchestra and band. The show was Directed and Produced by <a href="/wiki/Kenneth_Ehrlich" title="Kenneth Ehrlich">Ken Ehrlich</a> with the creative production from Celine's long time Lighting Designer Yves Aucoin and Sound Mixer/Designer, Dennis Savage.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-press_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-press-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Moment_Factory" title="Moment Factory">Moment Factory</a> will be producing the visual elements for the show (the company previously worked with Nine Inch Nails and DJ Tiesto). The show does not contain the dancers and <a href="/wiki/Cirque_du_Soleil" title="Cirque du Soleil">Cirque du Soleil</a> style choreography seen in Dion's previous show at the Colosseum, <a href="/wiki/A_New_Day..." title="A New Day...">A New Day</a>.</p>
<p>Dion announced her Vegas comeback during an appearance on <a href="/wiki/The_Oprah_Winfrey_Show" title="The Oprah Winfrey Show">The Oprah Winfrey Show</a>. Tickets went on sale that same week with extremely strong sales. The opening night show on 15 March was sold out within minutes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14"><span>[</span>14<span>]</span></a></sup> Rehearsals began on 17 January 2011, in Florida, and at the Colosseum starting on 17 February 2011 when Dion moved back to Vegas to begin preparations for the new show.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pdf_13-1"><a href="#cite_note-pdf-13"><span>[</span>13<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>Additionally, it was reported in January 2011 that ticket sales for the show had already passed the $10 million mark, marking the fastest sale in the history of the venue. Caesars Palace President, Gary Selesner stated that the first lot of shows through 17 April will be completely sold out.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15"><span>[</span>15<span>]</span></a></sup></p>

<p>Several concerts have been professionally filmed for both television and promotional use. The premiere concert on 15 March 2011 was filmed in its entirety. Excerpts from this concert were shown in the documentary "Celine: 3 Boys and a New Show", which was broadcast in early October 2011 on the <a href="/wiki/Oprah_Winfrey_Network_(U.S._TV_channel)" title="Oprah Winfrey Network (U.S. TV channel)">Oprah Winfrey Network</a>, and also various advertisements for the residency. The show of 17 April 2011 was also filmed in its entirety and excerpts of "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/All_By_Myself" title="All By Myself">All By Myself</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/My_Heart_Will_Go_On" title="My Heart Will Go On">My Heart Will Go On</a>" were shown in "Celine: 3 Boys and a New Show".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16"><span>[</span>16<span>]</span></a></sup> "<a href="/wiki/Open_Arms_(Journey_song)" title="Open Arms (Journey song)">Open Arms</a>" from the concert of 26 March was broadcast on <a href="/wiki/American_Broadcasting_Company" title="American Broadcasting Company">American Broadcasting Company</a> during the 2011 <a href="/wiki/MDA_Show_of_Strength" title="MDA Show of Strength">MDA Show of Strength</a> telethon on 4 September 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17"><span>[</span>17<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>On 19 June 2011, "<a href="/wiki/Because_You_Loved_Me" title="Because You Loved Me">Because You Loved Me</a>" was broadcast at the <a href="/wiki/63rd_Primetime_Emmy_Awards" title="63rd Primetime Emmy Awards">63rd Primetime Emmy Awards</a>. The song was filmed one day prior on 18 June.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18"><span>[</span>18<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>On 15 January 2012, Celine held a special concert supporting "Play without Pain: Children's Sickle Cell Benefit." The entire concert was filmed and excerpts of "<a href="/wiki/Open_Arms_(Journey_song)" title="Open Arms (Journey song)">Open Arms</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/Lullabye_(Goodnight,_My_Angel)" title="Lullabye (Goodnight, My Angel)">Lullabye (Goodnight, My Angel)</a>" were shown in the behind the scenes video of the event. A 47 second excerpt of "<a href="/wiki/My_Heart_Will_Go_On" title="My Heart Will Go On">My Heart Will Go On</a>" from this concert was sold to various news outlets following Celine's sudden announcement that all future concerts would be cancelled in August 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19"><span>[</span>19<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>A live video of "<a href="/wiki/My_Heart_Will_Go_On" title="My Heart Will Go On">My Heart Will Go On</a>" was leaked on <a href="/wiki/YouTube" title="YouTube">YouTube</a> on 29 July 2012, and was officially released on 31 July. The footage in this video was filmed on 1, 2 and 3 July 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20"><span>[</span>20<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>Celine's performance of "<a href="/wiki/Loved_Me_Back_to_Life" title="Loved Me Back to Life">Loved Me Back to Life</a>" on 30 December 2013 was filmed and was broadcast on <a href="/wiki/Entertainment_Tonight_Canada" title="Entertainment Tonight Canada">Entertainment Tonight Canada</a> during their New Year's Eve special on 31 December.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-21"><a href="#cite_note-21"><span>[</span>21<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Critical_reception">Critical reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Celine_(residency_show)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Critical reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The show received many positive reviews from music critics. <a href="/wiki/USA_Today" title="USA Today">USA Today</a> wrote, "Dion isn't here to perform. She's here to kill it. Again… she isn't aiming for applause. She's looking for rapture."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-22"><a href="#cite_note-22"><span>[</span>22<span>]</span></a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Las_Vegas_Sun" title="Las Vegas Sun">Las Vegas Sun</a> wrote, "A magnificent masterpiece! Celine has single-handedly redefined class and elegance in onstage performances. Her voice is sent from the angels."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-23"><a href="#cite_note-23"><span>[</span>23<span>]</span></a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Montreal_Gazette" title="Montreal Gazette">Montreal Gazette</a> wrote, "Dion magnificent in new Las Vegas show. One hell of a show!...Jaw-dropping... Grandiose, yet intimate…Pure entertainment of the highest order. Worth every penny!" <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-24"><a href="#cite_note-24"><span>[</span>24<span>]</span></a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Richard_Ouzounian" title="Richard Ouzounian">Richard Ouzounian</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Toronto_Star" title="Toronto Star">Toronto Star</a> headlined his article as, "Celine Dion's Vegas show more spectacular than ever," and claimed, "If her last show sold out for five years, this one should make it for ten." <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-25"><a href="#cite_note-25"><span>[</span>25<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Pollstar_Data">Pollstar Data</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Celine_(residency_show)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Pollstar Data">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>At the end of 2011 <a href="/wiki/Pollstar" title="Pollstar">Pollstar</a> announced that Celine was number 10 on their Top 25 North American Tours gross of $41.2 million.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pollstarpro.com_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-pollstarpro.com-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>At the end of 2012 Pollstar announced that Celine was number 29 on their Top 50 Worldwide Tours with a gross of $36.5 million.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-26"><a href="#cite_note-26"><span>[</span>26<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>At the end of 2013 Pollstar announced that Celine was number 23 on their Top 25 North American Tours list with a gross of $38.8million and number 25 on their Worldwide Tours (this included the number from the <a href="/wiki/Sans_attendre_Tour" title="Sans attendre Tour">Sans attendre Tour</a>.)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ReferenceA_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-ReferenceA-7"><span>[</span>7<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-27"><a href="#cite_note-27"><span>[</span>27<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>Pollstar announced during their 2014 Mid Year Tour Gross's Celine was at number 7 on their Top 100 North American Tours List and number 28 on the Top 100 Worldwide tours list with a gross of $21.1 million.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-28"><a href="#cite_note-28"><span>[</span>28<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-29"><a href="#cite_note-29"><span>[</span>29<span>]</span></a></sup></p>

<p>Notes</p>
<ol>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Michael_Jackson" title="Michael Jackson">Michael Jackson</a> tribute was replaced by "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rolling_in_the_deep" title="Rolling in the deep">Rolling in the deep</a>" from June 9th 2012 up to August 30th 2013 and was performed after "<a href="/wiki/All_by_Myself" title="All by Myself">All by Myself</a>."</li>
<li>During September 1st 2013 – February 22nd 2014 "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rolling_in_the_deep" title="Rolling in the deep">Rolling in the deep</a>" was replaced by "<a href="/wiki/Loved_Me_Back_to_Life_(song)" title="Loved Me Back to Life (song)">Loved Me Back to Life</a>."</li>
<li>On select dates, "Overjoyed" was replaced by "<a href="/wiki/The_Prayer_(Celine_Dion_and_Andrea_Bocelli_song)" title="The Prayer (Celine Dion and Andrea Bocelli song)">The Prayer</a>" (virtual duet with Stevie Wonder.)</li>
<li>"<a href="/wiki/Ne_me_quitte_pas" title="Ne me quitte pas">Ne me quitte pas</a>" was replaced by "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/S%27il_Suffisait_d%27Aimer" title="S'il Suffisait d'Aimer">S'il Suffisait d'Aimer</a>" from June 17th 2014 up to July 4th 2014. On July 29th 2014 Dion did not perform any French song</li>
</ol>
