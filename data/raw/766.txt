<section class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
<a id="dd_start"></a><p style="text-align: justify;">FLASH Entertainment confirmed they have come to an agreement to expand the du Arena slightly to offer small quantities of tickets for the Rolling Stones 14 On Fire Tour, presented by Eastspring Investments (#StonesOnFire) at the du Arena, Yas Island, Abu Dhabi on Friday, 21 February (#StonesAbuDhabi).</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">This measure was taken in response to the many thousands of requests for tickets that have come in since the show reached sell out on Thursday 30th January.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">A limited number of tickets will be available in all categories, which will include General Admission, Grandstand, Golden Circle and Fire Pit.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">A limited number of Early Bird tickets will also be available – except in Grandstand – starting at 395AED in General Admission, and will also be available in Golden Circle and Fire Pit categories. All tickets will go on sale to the general public at 12pm on Thursday, 6th February at ticketmaster.ae and all Virgin Megastores across the UAE.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The 14 ON FIRE tour produced by AEG Live will see Mick Jagger, Keith Richards, Charlie Watts and Ronnie Wood head back out on the road after a mammoth run of concerts in the U.S and the U.K in 2013, an exhilarating and critically acclaimed celebration of five decades of the Rolling Stones bringing their iconic music and groundbreaking stage shows to audiences around the globe.</p>
