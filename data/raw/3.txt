<p><b>A Tale of Two Cities</b> is a 1980 television film directed by <a href="/wiki/Jim_Goddard" title="Jim Goddard">Jim Goddard</a>, and based on the <a href="/wiki/Charles_Dickens" title="Charles Dickens">Charles Dickens</a> novel set in the <a href="/wiki/French_Revolution" title="French Revolution">French Revolution</a>. The film stars <a href="/wiki/Chris_Sarandon" title="Chris Sarandon">Chris Sarandon</a> playing dual roles as two characters who are in love with the same woman.</p>
