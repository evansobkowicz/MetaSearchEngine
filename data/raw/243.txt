<h1 itemprop="headline ">The Little Prince by Antoine de Saint Exupéry - quiz</h1>
<p class="stand-first-alone" id="stand-first">Insanely popular, with over a million copies sold every year, The Little Prince is a classic children's story telling the adventures of the boy who fell to earth. On the book's 70th anniversary, and with a new film by Universal pictures recently announced, we thought it was high time we tested your knowledge. Will your score make you look like a prince or a pauper?</p>
