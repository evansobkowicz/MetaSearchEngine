<p class="full-note">
Alice falls through the rabbit hole. In the first part of the stunt, a stuntwoman falls 20' through a 2 ½' opening to a porta pit. In the second part of the stunt, a she is on wires, falls 25' to an Aussie trampoline and bounces to a porta pit. In the third part of the stunt, a stuntwoman jumps 20' thru a breakaway ceiling onto porta pits. Then, the stuntwoman was hung 12' on a quick release, cut away to a curved set piece and hit a floor with no padding. CGI was used throughout the sequence of falls and landings.            </p>
