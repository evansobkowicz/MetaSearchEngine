<h3>How does it work?</h3>
<p>Wouldn't it be fun if you could call your best friend and tell her you're hanging out with Kim Kardashian? Barack Obama? How about calling your boss as The Terminator? Well now you can! When you use CelebDial with the phone app on your texting device, you can click buttons on a web page that play sound clips of your favorite celebrity's voice, directly into your call. CelebDial also records calls so you can share them with your Facebook friends.</p>
<p>CelebDial is in <span class="label label-warning">public beta</span> and 100% free for the time being. Enjoy this while it lasts because we'll probably change our minds once we see our phone bill.</p>
