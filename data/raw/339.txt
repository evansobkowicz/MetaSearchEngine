<div class="grid3col">
<div class="column first">
<p>The creators of the beloved &amp;ldquo;Toy Story&amp;rdquo; films re&amp;mdash;open the toy box and bring moviegoers back to the delightful world of Woody, Buzz and our favorite gang of toy characters in TOY STORY 3. Woody and Buzz had accepted that their owner Andy would grow up someday, but what happens when that day arrives? In the third installment, Andy is preparing to depart for college, leaving his loyal toys troubled about their uncertain future. Lee Unkrich (co&amp;mdash;director of &amp;ldquo;Toy Story 2&amp;rdquo; and &amp;ldquo;Finding Nemo&amp;rdquo;) directs this highly anticipated film, and Michael Arndt, the Academy Award&amp;reg;&amp;mdash;winning screenwriter of &amp;ldquo;Little Miss Sunshine,&amp;rdquo; brings his unique talents and comedic sensibilities to the proceedings. </p>
