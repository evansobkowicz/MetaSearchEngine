<h2>The Catcher in the Rye Challenge</h2>
<p class="breadcrumb"> <a href="/page.cfm/scholarships" title="Scholarship">Scholarship</a> &gt; The Catcher in the Rye Challenge  </p>
<div class="entry"><h4><strong style="font-size: 1.2em;">About the Scholarship<a href="http://astore.amazon.com/commoknowls07-20/detail/0316769487" target="_blank"><img alt="" src="http://blog.cksf.org/assets/content/rye_catcher.jpg" style="width: 200px; height: 279px; float: right; margin: 10px;"/></a></strong></h4>
<hr/>
<p>CKSF's The Catcher in the Rye Challenge provides people, of all ages, a chance to win a scholarship by taking several online quizzes based off of J. D. Salinger's <a href="http://astore.amazon.com/commoknowls07-20/detail/0316769487" target="_blank"><u><em>The Catcher in the Rye</em></u></a>.</p>
<h4><strong>Eligibility</strong></h4>
<hr/>
<p>Anyone in the United States  </p>
<h4><strong>Awards</strong></h4>
<hr/>
<p>Electronic Amazon gift cards will be awarded to the top ten scorers.</p>

<p> </p>
<h4><strong>How it Works</strong></h4>
<hr/>
<p>Participants must register with <a href="http://www.cksf.org/" target="_blank">CKSF</a> to take the scholarship quizzes. The Challenge Round is split up into 4 quizzes. Scores from each quiz are added together for each participant and the top 25% of scorers will be advanced to the Championship Round. The top ten scorers at the end of the Championship Round will receive electronic Amazon gift cards.</p>
<h4><strong><strong>Quiz Dates</strong></strong></h4>
<hr/>
<p><b>Challenge Round:</b><br/>
Quiz 1 (Chapters 1-7): 8/4/2013 - 9/1/2013<br/>
Quiz 2 (Chapters 8-14): 8/11/2013 - 9/1/2013<br/>
Quiz 3 (Chapters 15-20): 8/18/2013 - 9/1/2013<br/>
Quiz 4 (Chapters 21-26): 8/25/2013 - 9/1/2013<br/>
<i>Top 25% of scorers advance to the Championship Round</i></p>
<p><b>Championship Round:</b> 9/4/2013 - 9/6/2013</p>
<h4><strong><strong>Quiz Details</strong></strong></h4>
<hr/>
<p>To participate, you must register with the Common Knowledge Scholarship Foundation at www.cksf.org. For all CKSF scholarship quizzes, 500 points are awarded  for each correct answer and 1 point is deducted for each second taken to complete a question. Results are available immediately to each participant and the overall ranking is available within three hours under the "Results" tab at www.cksf.org. </p>
