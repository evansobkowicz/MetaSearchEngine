<h1 class="title">On This Day…</h1>
<div id="content-body-inner">
<div class="node nodetype-news clearfix" id="node-225900">
<div class="datetime day-6">
<span class="news-date">2014/07/26</span>
<span class="news-time">2:00AM</span>
<span class="news-flag"><img alt="Canada (English)" height="11" src="/sites/all/modules/custom/sony_global/flags/ca.png" title="Canada (English)" width="16"/></span>
</div>
<div class="rhs">
<div class="content clear-block">
<!--paging_filter--><p>On this day last year, Celine and René were invested in the Order of Canada during a ceremony at the Residence of the Governor General at the Citadelle of Quebec in Quebec City. His Excellency the Right Honourable David Johnston, Governor General of Canada, presented the insignia of Companion of the Order of Canada to Céline and the insignia of Member of the Order of Canada to René. They were presented with these insignias in recognition of their strong values shared by Canadians and promoted throughout the world.<br/>
<br/><img src="/sites/cdion/files/imagecache/preview/gg2013-0310-001.jpg"/></p>
