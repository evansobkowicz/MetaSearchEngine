<p><strong>Nick Cannon</strong> and estranged wife <strong>Mariah Carey</strong> definitely aren't on good terms!</p>
<p>According to a new report, Nick went bonkers when he heard his baby mama's song playing at Chris Paul’s L.A. charity bowling tournament in LA last night.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.intouchweekly.com/posts/nick-cannon-and-mariah-carey-sued-by-former-nanny-report-50491">Read more: Nick Cannon and Mariah Carey Sued By Former Nanny (REPORT)</a></p>
<p>A source tells 'Us Weekly' the 'America's Got Talent' host "looked irritated," adding, "He was cringing ... Nick had a publicist go over to the DJ and demand he stop playing Mariah's tracks."</p>
<p><img alt=" Nick Cannon Mariah Carey " src="http://images.lifeandstylemag.com/uploads/images/file/10892/nick-mariah.jpg?fit=crop&amp;w=680" title=" Nick Cannon Mariah Carey "> <p style="text-align: center">Nick and Mariah during happier times in 2012. (Photo Credit: Getty Images)</p></img></p>
<p>Yikes...</p>
<p>Nick and Mariah, who wed in April 2008 and share three-year-old twins Moroccan and Monroe, called it quits last August. The 'Wild 'N Out' star reportedly filed for divorce last month.</p>
<p>'<a href="http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/news/nick-cannon-demanded-dj-to-stop-playing-mariah-carey-music-flipped-201572">Us Weekly</a>' reported the news.</p>
