<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en"><span dir="auto">Bobby Singh (cinematographer)</span></h1>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><p><b>Bobby Singh</b> (1974 - December 25, 2012) was a noted Indian cinematographer who worked in <a href="/wiki/Bollywood" title="Bollywood">Bollywood</a>, most known for films like <i><a href="/wiki/Gangster_(2006_film)" title="Gangster (2006 film)">Gangster</a></i> (2006), <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Life_in_a_Metro" title="Life in a Metro">Life in a Metro</a></i> (2007), <i><a href="/wiki/The_Dirty_Picture" title="The Dirty Picture">The Dirty Picture</a></i> (2011) and <i><a href="/wiki/Queen_(film)" title="Queen (film)">Queen</a></i> (2014).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> He died following an asthma attack in Goa on December 25, 2012 at the age of 38.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-India_Today_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-India_Today-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bobby_Singh_(cinematographer)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Singh started his career in 1989, as an assistant cameraman in <i><a href="/wiki/Shehzaade" title="Shehzaade">Shehzaade</a></i> directed by <a href="/wiki/Raj_N._Sippy" title="Raj N. Sippy">Raj N. Sippy</a>, was assistant cameramen in <i><a href="/wiki/Sarfarosh" title="Sarfarosh">Sarfarosh</a></i> (1999) and later also did additional photography for <i><a href="/wiki/Ghajini_(2008_film)" title="Ghajini (2008 film)">Ghajini</a></i> (2008) directed by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A._R._Murugadoss" title="A. R. Murugadoss">A. R. Murugadoss</a>. Eventually, he made his debut as an independent cinematographer with crime thriller <i><a href="/wiki/Gangster_(2006_film)" title="Gangster (2006 film)">Gangster</a></i> (2006) directed by <a href="/wiki/Anurag_Basu" title="Anurag Basu">Anurag Basu</a>, and starring Shiney Ahuja, Emraan Hashmi and debutant Kangna Ranaut. He worked again with Basu in acclaimed <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Life_in_a_Metro" title="Life in a Metro">Life in a Metro</a></i> (2007). His next big film was <a href="/wiki/Milan_Luthria" title="Milan Luthria">Milan Luthria</a>'s National Film Award-winning biopic <i><a href="/wiki/The_Dirty_Picture" title="The Dirty Picture">The Dirty Picture</a></i> (2011) starring <a href="/wiki/Vidya_Balan" title="Vidya Balan">Vidya Balan</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-India_Today_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-India_Today-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rediff_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-rediff-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>He died suddenly following an asthma attack on December 25, 2012, at the age of 38. At the time, he was on a holiday in Goa, with wife and son.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup> At the time of his death he had completed the shooting of <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Special_Chabbis" title="Special Chabbis">Special Chabbis</a></i>, which was released posthumously in February 2013. He had shot 90 percent of Kangana Ranaut starrer <i><a href="/wiki/Queen_(film)" title="Queen (film)">Queen</a></i> directed by <a href="/wiki/Vikas_Bahl" title="Vikas Bahl">Vikas Bahl</a>, which was eventually released in 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rediff_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-rediff-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bobby_Singh_(cinematographer)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
