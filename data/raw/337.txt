<p><b>John Charles Morris</b> (born October 2, 1984) is an American <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actor</a> and <a href="/wiki/Voice_acting" title="Voice acting">voice actor</a>. He is best known for his role as <a href="/wiki/List_of_Toy_Story_characters#Andy_Davis" title="List of Toy Story characters">Andy Davis</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Toy_Story_(franchise)" title="Toy Story (franchise)"><i>Toy Story</i> film trilogy</a>. He currently resides in Northern <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>. He graduated in 2007 from the University of California, Los Angeles's School of Theater, Film &amp; Television.</p>
<p>When auditioning for the role of Andy he brought some of his <a href="/wiki/X-Men" title="X-Men">X-Men</a> action figures and made voices for them, the Pixar staff loved it and gave him the part.</p>
<p>Morris was brought back on to <i><a href="/wiki/Toy_Story_3" title="Toy Story 3">Toy Story 3</a></i> by the request of the director <a href="/wiki/Lee_Unkrich" title="Lee Unkrich">Lee Unkrich</a>. Unkrich described the process by which he went to get Morris on the <i>Toy Story 3</i> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Blu-ray" title="Blu-ray">Blu-ray</a>/<a href="/wiki/DVD" title="DVD">DVD</a> detailing how he called him to ask him to reprise his role of <a href="/wiki/List_of_Toy_Story_characters#Andy_Davis" title="List of Toy Story characters">Andy</a> (from the original <i><a href="/wiki/Toy_Story" title="Toy Story">Toy Story</a></i>), and after hearing Morris' voicemail greeting, Unkrich knew immediately that Morris would again be playing Andy.</p>
