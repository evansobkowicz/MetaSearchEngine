<p><b>Alexander Jerome Gould</b> (born May 4, 1994) is an American actor and voice artist, best known for voicing Nemo in <i><a href="/wiki/Finding_Nemo" title="Finding Nemo">Finding Nemo</a></i>, Twitch in <i><a href="/wiki/How_to_Eat_Fried_Worms_(film)" title="How to Eat Fried Worms (film)">How to Eat Fried Worms</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/List_of_Weeds_characters#Shane_Botwin" title="List of Weeds characters">Shane Botwin</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Weeds_(TV_series)" title="Weeds (TV series)">Weeds</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexander_Gould&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gould was born in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, to Tom and Valerie Gould. He has two younger sisters, Emma and <a href="/wiki/Kelly_Gould" title="Kelly Gould">Kelly</a>, who are also actors. Gould practices Judaism, and is active in the <a href="/wiki/Conservative_Judaism" title="Conservative Judaism">Conservative Jewish</a> youth group <a href="/wiki/United_Synagogue_Youth" title="United Synagogue Youth">USY</a> (he was president of his local USY synagogue chapter during his senior year of high school). He participated in <a href="/wiki/Nativ_College_Leadership_Program_in_Israel" title="Nativ College Leadership Program in Israel">Nativ</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Gap_year" title="Gap year">gap year</a> program, where he spent ten months living in Israel, including time in the <a href="/wiki/Negev" title="Negev">Negev</a> desert, teaching English to elementary school children.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-timer1_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-timer1-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>Gould is a student at <a href="/wiki/Clark_University" title="Clark University">Clark University</a> in Massachusetts.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-timer1_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-timer1-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexander_Gould&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gould made his acting debut at the age of 6 in the episode of <i><a href="/wiki/Freaks_and_Geeks" title="Freaks and Geeks">Freaks and Geeks</a></i> where Lindsey gets high and then has to babysit his character of Ronnie. Gould made his feature film debut in <i><a href="/wiki/They_(2002_film)" title="They (2002 film)">They</a></i>, and is best remembered for voicing the title character of <a href="/wiki/Walt_Disney_Pictures" title="Walt Disney Pictures">Disney</a>/<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pixar_Animation_Studios" title="Pixar Animation Studios">Pixar</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Finding_Nemo" title="Finding Nemo">Finding Nemo</a></i>. On television, Gould played the role of Shane Botwin on the <a href="/wiki/Showtime_(TV_network)" title="Showtime (TV network)">Showtime</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Weeds_(TV_series)" title="Weeds (TV series)">Weeds</a></i>, as well as guest-starring on such series as <i><a href="/wiki/Ally_McBeal" title="Ally McBeal">Ally McBeal</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Malcolm_in_the_Middle" title="Malcolm in the Middle">Malcolm in the Middle</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Law_%26_Order:_Special_Victims_Unit" title="Law &amp; Order: Special Victims Unit">Law &amp; Order: Special Victims Unit</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Supernatural_(U.S._TV_series)" title="Supernatural (U.S. TV series)">Supernatural</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Pushing_Daisies" title="Pushing Daisies">Pushing Daisies</a></i>. He starred as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/David_Collins_(Dark_Shadows)" title="David Collins (Dark Shadows)">David Collins</a> in the WB remake of the soap opera <i><a href="/wiki/Dark_Shadows" title="Dark Shadows">Dark Shadows</a></i>, but its pilot episode was rejected by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/WB_Television_Network" title="WB Television Network">WB Television Network</a> and never aired.</p>
