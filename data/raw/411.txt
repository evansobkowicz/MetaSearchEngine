<p>The first trailer for <strong><em>The Dark Knight Rises</em></strong> Blu-ray has been released in the form of a 90-second sizzle reel.  The clip highlights some of the more exciting moments from <strong>Christopher Nolan</strong>’s Batman Trilogy closer, and also gives us our first look at some of the disc’s special features, including the documentary on the history of the Batmobile.  We recently got a peek at the different <a href="http://collider.com/dark-knight-rises-blu-ray-target-best-buy-images/200096/" target="_blank">exclusive packaging for the film from Target and Best Buy</a>, with Best Buy’s version boasting an exclusive documentary called <strong><em>Reborn</em></strong>.  All versions of the Blu-ray <a href="http://collider.com/dark-knight-rises-blu-ray-images/198459/" target="_blank">will include</a> the Batmobile documentary, a featurette called <strong><em>The Journey of Bruce Wayne</em></strong>, and a 17-featurette in-depth look at the making of the film called <strong><em>Ending the Knight</em></strong>.</p>
<p>Hit the jump to watch the trailer.  <a href="http://collider.com/tag/the-dark-knight-rises" target="_blank"><strong><em>The Dark Knight Rises</em></strong></a> will be available on Blu-ray, DVD, and digital download on December 4th.<span id="more-203167"></span></p>

<p>Here’s the synopsis for <strong><em>The Dark Knight Rises</em></strong> Blu-ray:</p>
<blockquote><p>The Dark Knight Rises” is the epic conclusion to filmmaker Christopher Nolan’s Dark Knight trilogy. It has been eight years since Batman vanished into the night, turning, in that instant, from hero to fugitive. Assuming the blame for the death of D.A. Harvey Dent, the Dark Knight sacrificed everything for what he and Commissioner Gordon both hoped was the greater good. For a time the lie worked, as criminal activity in Gotham City was crushed under the weight of the anti-crime Dent Act. But everything will change with the arrival of a cunning cat burglar with a mysterious agenda. Far more dangerous, however, is the emergence of Bane, a masked terrorist whose ruthless plans for Gotham drive Bruce out of his self-imposed exile. But even if he dons the cape and cowl again, Batman may be no match for Bane.</p>
<p>Own it on Blu-ray Combo Pack or Digital Download December 4</p></blockquote>
