<p>MUCH CHILLIER FOR ASHEVILLE ON TUESDAY. NOW TO THE BIG CATS IN THE STUDIO. OUR GUESTS ARE GETTING READY TO TAKE AUDIENCES TO THE LAND OF NARNIA. THEY'RE PART OF A THEATRE KNOWN FOR BRINGING STORIES FROM THE BIBLE TO LIFE. LET'S WELCOME OUR GUESTS FROM THE ACADEMY OF ARTS LOGOS THEATRE. NICOLE AND KNOW WHAT ARE BOTH WITH THE THEATER. IT IS A FAMILY AFFAIR IS IN IT? -- ISN'T IT? IT IS. MY PARENTS STARTED IT IN 1971. FOR ABOUT SEVEN YEARS, WE HAVE HAD THE THEATER HERE IN GREENVILLE. AS WE TAKE A LOOK AT WEBSITE, WE CAN SEE YOU ARE MORE THAN A THEATER. YOU ARE A MINISTRY. WE HAVE ABOUT FIVE DIFFERENT ASPECTS OF OUR MINISTRY. WE HAVE A TWO-YEAR COLLEGE PROGRAM SOON TO BE A BACHELORS PROGRAM AS WELL. WE HAVE A TRAVELING MINISTRY. WE HAVE A FILM PROGRAM WE ARE RAISING RIGHT NOW. WE HAVE A THEATER IN A NUMBER OF THINGS GOING ON. WE STAY BUSY ALL THE TIME. NOW YOU HAVE THIS GIGANTIC CAT. THIS IS A FAMILIAR FACE. TELL US WHAT HE IS MADE OUT OF. ALUMINUM AND A LOT OF PLASTIC. WE TOOK REAL WOOD AND VACUUM FORMED IT OVER TOP OF THAT TO GIVE A VERY REALISTIC LOOK. THAT WAS SOMETHING OUR TECHNICAL DIRECTOR WORKED ON WITH A COUPLE OF OTHER PEOPLE FOR ALMOST SIX MONTHS. IT TAKES THREE ACTORS TO RUN HIM. NOBODY FROM ANY PART OF THE OTHER END -- THAT'S GREAT. LET US TAKE A LOOK AT SOME OF THE VIDEO FROM THE PRODUCTION. IT IS SPECTACULAR. IT HAS BEEN ENJOYABLE TO BRING THE STORY TO LIFE. WE WANT TO MAKE SURE WE ARE TRUE TO THE BOOK. WE HAVE 130 IN THE CAST. WE WORK ON IT AND GET TOGETHER AND BUILT A BEAVER DAM. THE WOLVES CAN CLIMB UP ON THERE AND GO INTO IT AND REBUT A PART -- AND RIP IT APART. THIS WAS ACTUALLY A STORY I SAID I WOULD NEVER DIRECT BECAUSE OF ALL THE DIFFERENT THINGS YOU SEE IN YOUR MIND WHEN YOU READ. IT WAS FUN TO SEE IT COME TO LIFE AND TO GET INTO THAT IMAGINATION WORLD. IT IS A HUGE TEAM EFFORT. PEOPLE ARE UNDER THE STAGE SHOOTING UP THINGS AND EVERYTHING. LAST YEAR, IT TOOK US PROBABLY ALMOST A FULL YEAR TO GET EVERYTHING READY. WE REHEARSED FOR A FEW WEEKS. MY WIFE IS FANTASTIC APPALLING THINGS TOGETHER IN AN EXCELLENT WAY. WE WISH OLD -- WE WERE SOLD OUT LAST YEAR AND WE LOOK FORWARD TO DO IT AGAIN. DO PEOPLE HAVE A CHANCE TO SEE HIM UP CLOSE? THEY CAN GET A BACKSTAGE PASS AND SEE HIM UP CLOSE AND TAKE PICTURES WITH HIM. IT IS AN INCREDIBLE THING TO SEE THE KIDS SEEING ASLAN. MAKES YOU FEEL LIKE A KID. THERE IS A POINT WHERE I AM SPEAKING BY NEVIN ELVISH LANGUAGE TO HIM AND WE ARE BARTERING. HE SPEAKS BACK TO ME AND IT IS VERY INTIMIDATING. I BET. THIS IS MAGNIFICENT. LET US ARE MIND EVERYONE WHEN THE SHOW TAKES PLACE. 10 PERFORMANCES AND THEY WANT TO CHECK THE WEBSITE IS THE TIMES VARY DEPENDING ON THE DATE. FEBRUARY 13 THROUGH THE 28TH. PRICES ARE LITTLE DIFFERENT FIRST STUDENTS. ALL DIFFERENT AGES ARE WELCOME. THE THEATER IS DOWNTOWN TAYLORS NEAR THE OLD ELEMENTARY SCHOOL. WE HAVE A REVOLVING FLOOR WE BUILT OURSELVES THAT MAKES THE STAGE REVOLVES. THERE IS SOME MAGIC IN IT.</p></div>
