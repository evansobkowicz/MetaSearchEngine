<section>
<p>Warcraft movie news are very scarce, until director Duncan Jones hops on Twitter to talk to fans and tease us a bit more.</p>
<p><span id="more-511931"></span><a href="http://assets.vg247.com/current//2015/02/warcraft-movie-header.jpg"><img alt="warcraft movie header" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-511934" height="324" src="http://assets.vg247.com/current//2015/02/warcraft-movie-header-600x324.jpg" width="600"/></a></p>
<p>Duncan Jones, director of the upcoming Warcraft movie has been answering fan questions and teasing a few details about the 2016 flick on Twitter.</p>
<p>First, regarding the amount of CGI that will be used in the film, Jones had this to say:</p>
<blockquote class="twitter-tweet" lang="en"><p>.<a href="https://twitter.com/tcwoolley">@tcwoolley</a> I would say that we fit somewhere between Planet of the Apes, Fellowship of the Ring and Avatar.</p>
<p>— Duncan Jones (@ManMadeMoon) <a href="https://twitter.com/ManMadeMoon/status/563496576119631872">February 6, 2015</a></p></blockquote>
<p></p>
<p>He then revealed that he’s looking into the idea of two separate trailers. One for the Alliance and another for the Horde.</p>
<blockquote class="twitter-tweet" lang="en"><p>.<a href="https://twitter.com/pabosher">@pabosher</a> I love that idea. Have pitched it myself. We'll have to see…</p>
<p>— Duncan Jones (@ManMadeMoon) <a href="https://twitter.com/ManMadeMoon/status/563497877939290114">February 6, 2015</a></p></blockquote>
<p></p>
<p>Jones also talked about how the movie’s theme is coming together, and other few bits <a href="https://twitter.com/ManMadeMoon" target="_new">on his Twitter</a>.</p>
<p>The Warcraft movie is scheduled for March 2016.</p>
<p>Thanks, <a href="http://www.joblo.com/movie-news/duncan-jones-teases-separate-alliance-and-horde-trailers-for-warcraft-226" target="_new">JoBlo</a>.</p>
