<h1>Dark Knight Rises: Teaser Poster</h1>
<div class="author-details">
<img alt="Bchwood" class="avatar" height="48" src="http://vignette3.wikia.nocookie.net/common/avatars/0/04/3242493.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width/50?format=jpg" width="48"/> <span class="post-author"><a href="/wiki/User:Bchwood">Bchwood</a></span>
<span>July 12, 2011</span>
<span><a href="/wiki/User_blog:Bchwood">User blog:Bchwood</a></span>
</div>
</header>
<div class="WikiaArticle" id="WikiaArticle">
<div class="home-top-right-ads">
</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr mw-content-text" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="floatright"><a class="image image-thumbnail" href="http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/batman/images/7/74/TheDarkKnightRises_TeaserPoster.jpg/revision/latest?cb=20110712052302"><img alt="TheDarkKnightRises TeaserPoster" class="" data-image-key="TheDarkKnightRises_TeaserPoster.jpg" data-image-name="TheDarkKnightRises TeaserPoster.jpg" height="444" src="http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/batman/images/7/74/TheDarkKnightRises_TeaserPoster.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width/300?cb=20110712052302" width="300"/></a></div>
<p>Warner Bros. has unveiled the teaser poster for its summer 2012 tentpole <a href="/wiki/The_Dark_Knight_Rises" title="The Dark Knight Rises">The Dark Knight Rises</a>.
</p><p>The image, posted Monday at thedarkknightrises.com, features a view of <a href="/wiki/Gotham_City" title="Gotham City">Gotham City</a>, with the camera pointing upward at several skyscrapers as they appear to be collapsing but giving the discernible shape of a bat. No cast members in it, not even <a href="/wiki/Christian_Bale" title="Christian Bale">Christian Bale</a>. Warners previously released images of <a class="new" href="/wiki/Thomas_Hardy?action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Thomas Hardy (page does not exist)">Thomas Hardy</a> as the villain <a href="/wiki/Bane" title="Bane">Bane</a>, but this is the first poster image.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Gary_Oldman" title="Gary Oldman">Gary Oldman</a> will reprise his role as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Commissioner_Gordon" title="Commissioner Gordon">Commissioner Gordon</a>, while a slew of franchise newcomers also has joined the film, including Anne Hathaway, Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Marion Cotillard.
</p><p>What do you think of the poster? Pretty cool in my opinion.
</p>
