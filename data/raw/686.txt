<h2 class="rm">Biography</h2>
<h3 class="remove-top-margin"><span class="locality">Stockholm</span>, <span class="country-name">Sweden</span>    
    (<span itemprop="foundingDate">1970</span> – 1982)</h3> </hgroup>
</header>
<div class="wiki">
<div class="wiki-abstract" data-require="music/wiki" id="wikiAbstract">
<div class="wiki-text">
 ABBA was a <a class="bbcode_tag" href="http://www.last.fm/tag/pop" rel="tag">pop</a> music group that formed in Stockholm, Sweden in November 1970. The band consisted of <a class="bbcode_artist" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Anni-Frid+Lyngstad">Anni-Frid Lyngstad</a> (<a class="bbcode_artist" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Frida">Frida</a>), <a class="bbcode_artist" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Bj%C3%B6rn+Ulvaeus">Björn Ulvaeus</a>, <a class="bbcode_artist" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Benny+Andersson">Benny Andersson</a>, and <a class="bbcode_artist" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Agnetha+F%C3%A4ltskog">Agnetha Fältskog</a>. (See also <a class="bbcode_artist" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Bj%C3%B6rn+Ulvaeus+&amp;+Benny+Andersson">Björn Ulvaeus &amp; Benny Andersson</a>, as well as Benny’s previous band <a class="bbcode_artist" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Hep+Stars">Hep Stars</a>.)<br/>
<br/>
They topped the charts worldwide from 1972 to 1982 with eight studio albums, achieving twenty-six #1 singles and numerous awards. They also won the 1974 Eurovision Song Contest with “<span class="spotify-inline-play-button inline-play-button" data-uri="spotify:track:04rW2GITOOJQpD7rvjyMmo"></span><a class="bbcode_track" href="http://www.last.fm/music/ABBA/_/Waterloo" title="ABBA – Waterloo">Waterloo</a>”.
 <span class="hide"> <br/><br/> A <a class="bbcode_tag" href="http://www.last.fm/tag/jukebox%20musical" rel="tag">jukebox musical</a> based on the music of ABBA titled <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mamma_Mia!" rel="nofollow">Mamma Mia!</a> opened in London’s West End in 1999 and has since become one of the most popular musicals in the world. In 2008, a movie version was made with <a class="bbcode_artist" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Meryl+Streep">Meryl Streep</a> among the cast.<br/>
<br/>
The recent <a href="http://rockhall.com/inductees/abba/" rel="nofollow">Rock and Roll Hall of Fame 2010 Induction Ceremony</a>, held on March 15 in New York, recognized the huge contribution of ABBA. Represented by Anni-Frid Lyngstad “Frida” and former husband/band member Benny Andersson, it was perhaps the crowning acknowledgment of the phenomenal success of Sweden’s most famous four.
 </span> </div>
