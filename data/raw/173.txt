<h2><a data-rel="fancybox" href="http://sullivanmovies.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Anne-of-Green-Gables-Audio-Book.jpg"><img alt="Anne of Green Gables Audio Book" class="size-full wp-image-8513 alignleft" height="372" src="http://sullivanmovies.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Anne-of-Green-Gables-Audio-Book.jpg" width="350"/></a>Synopsis</h2>
<p>Now is your chance to revisit Lucy Maud Montgomery’s classic story in a totally new way! Perfect for long car rides or trips, this audio book is read by the acclaimed Writer/Director of all the Anne of Green Gables films, Kevin Sullivan. The Anne of Green Gables Audio Book is great listening for the whole family and is a perfect addition to every Anne of Green Gables collection.</p>
<p><strong>Plot Summary:</strong> The moment an eleven-year-old, plucky orphan named Anne Shirley arrives at Green Gables, the lives of brother and sister, Matthew and Marilla Cuthbert, are changed forever. Anne is a skinny, self-possessed spirit with fiery, red hair, whose boundless imagination transports her from one adventure to the next. Perpetually in search of like-minded spirits, Anne’s journey to belong is met with many obstacles along the way. However, she is determined to say and do anything to convince her mild-mannered hosts to let her stay at Green Gables. Montgomery’s classic is set against the spectacular beauty of Prince Edward Island, Canada; a place that captures both Anne’s heart and the emotions of legions of readers around the world. The timeless stories and characters come to life, just as they do on page, in this audio-book feature.</p>
