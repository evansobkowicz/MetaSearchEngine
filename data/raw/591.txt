<div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>Just hours after <a href="http://www.billboard.com/artist/308786/madonna/chart">Madonna</a> took the <a href="http://www.billboard.com/grammys/2015">2015 Grammys stage</a> with a herd of masked minotaurs to perform "Living for Love," the pop icon released three more tracks from her forthcoming <em>Rebel Heart</em> album.</p>
<p>Now available for those who <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/rebel-heart/id953710965" target="_blank">pre-ordered the album</a> are "Hold Tight," "Joan of Arc" and her <a href="http://www.billboard.com/artist/4281953/chance-the-rapper/chart">Chance the Rapper</a>/Mike Tyson collaboration "Iconic."</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://www.billboard.com/articles/events/grammys-2015/6465577/madonna-living-for-love-grammys" target="_blank"><strong>Madonna Performs 'Living for Love' at the Grammys</strong></a></p>
<p>"Hold Tight" is the best of the bunch, an immediately familiar track with an epic, arresting chorus and martial drumming.</p>
<p>The studio version of "Joan of Arc" is a massive improvement on the illegally leaked demo that preceded news of the album itself. On the Grammy red carpet Sunday night, Madonna said the worst part of the <em>Rebel Heart</em> leaks was that fans heard incomplete/rejected versions of her new material. Nowhere is this more apparent than on "Joan of Arc," a solid album track that sounds 10 times better than the leaked version.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop-shop/6450641/mike-tyson-madonna-collaboration-benito-mussolini"><strong>Mike Tyson Explains Madonna Collabo, Their Long-time Friendship</strong></a></p>
<p>"Iconic," Madonna's Chance the Rapper and Mike Tyson collaboration, is just about as odd as you'd expect. Mike Tyson introduces the song with a boastful spoken word segment that might inspire hubristic listeners if not for the fact that it's actually a quote from blood-stained Fascist dictator Benito Mussolini.</p>
<p>Fortunately, the Axis shout-outs end there -- Chance doesn't reference <em>Mein Kampf</em> in his verse -- but the rest of the song is a fairly overwrought affair. Chance's verse is fire, but the intense, busy production ultimately adds up to very little.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.billboard.com/articles/events/grammys-2015/6465587/grammys-2015-madonna-butt-red-carpet-photo"><strong>Madonna Moons Grammy Red Carpet</strong></a></p>
<p>You can listen to the new <em>Rebel Heart</em> tracks if you <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/rebel-heart/id953710965" target="_blank">pre-order it on iTunes</a>, and watch Madonna's "Living for Love" <a href="https://www.billboard.com/articles/events/grammys-2015/6465577/madonna-living-for-love-grammys">Grammy performance here</a>.</p>
