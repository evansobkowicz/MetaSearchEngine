<div class="StoryContent">
		

	Theres no way in the world <i>Inception</i> will ever get a sequel.  Youre going to hear a lot of talk about the possibility of <i>Inception 2</i>.  In fact at one point <a href="http://www.thedailyrotation.com/christopher-nolan-to-film-inception-2-after-the-dark-knight-rises/">rumor</a> had it that Chris Nolan would begin shooting <i>Inception 2</i> after completing <i>The Dark Knight Rises</i>.  That never happened, and it never will.  <i>Inception 2</i> is an idea which will never even be considered, let alone actually get done.  <a href="http://www.cinemablend.com/news.php?tag=christopher%20nolan">Christopher Nolan</a> is too smart to permit it.  
<br><br>
The <a href="http://www.cinemablend.com/Inception-3752.html"><i>Inception</i></a> story, in spite of the <a href="http://www.cinemablend.com/new/Inception-Explained-Unraveling-The-Dream-Within-The-Dream-19615.html">much discussed</a> cliffhanger at the end, is clearly intended as a single, self-contained film.  The movie made Warner Bros. Pictures a ton of money and so the studio may want <i>Inception 2</i>, but right now Nolan has all the clout he needs to keep it from happening. Even if he didnt, hed never agree to do it, and theres no <i>Inception 2</i> without Christopher Nolan.  So forget it. Its hard enough to keep a dynamic filmmaker like this focused on making more Batman, which truly lends itself to more movies, without trying to strong-arm him into making sequels to things which would only be lessoned by them. 
<br><br>
Even the cast seems to know that the notion of doing <i>Inception 2</i> is ridiculous.  <a href="http://www.mtv.com">MTV</a> caught up with a lot of the primary cast on the <i>Inception</i> premiere red carpet and tried to start the sequel rumors going, but instead what they got was a bunch of stars who had no interest in talking about another one.  <a href="http://www.cinemablend.com/news.php?tag=cillian%20murphy">Cillian Murphy</a> told them, "I don't have the authority to speak on that."  
<br><br>
<a href="http://www.imdb.com/name/nm2438307/">Dileep Rao</a> who plays Yusef and Lukus Haas whos barely in the movie, tried to leave the <i>Inception 2</i> door open, but seriously, this is never happening no matter how many people try to turn their "in dreams anything can happen" quotes into sequel rumors. 
<br><br>
<i>Inception 2</i>?  No way it happens, and thats how it should be. Ignore anyone who tries to tell you otherwise, odds are theyre selling something. Instead, just enjoy discussing the movie we got.  With layers upon layers to examine, there's no shortage of material.  Here's a handy guide to get you started...
