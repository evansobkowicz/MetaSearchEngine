Unfortunately for the Instagram lingerie model, now that her ability to sing has publicly been called into question following a recent lip-sync scandal and embarrassing Rockefeller, even some of Mariah's fellow R&amp;B legends are coming out of the woodwork to take potshots. <p>In a recent <a href="https://www.facebook.com/nathanbmorris/posts/10153046683583255?pnref=story">Facebook</a> post Maraih's fellow 90s R&amp;B star let Carey know that she could have seen this whole thing coming if only she'd cared enough to pay attention:</p>
<p><iframe class="videocontent" height="360" src="http://cdnapi.kaltura.com/index.php/kwidget/wid/1_ve8ooflh/uiconf_id/6740162/st_cache/76583?&amp;" width="610">Unfortunately your browser does not support IFrames.</iframe></p>

<p>"Nick looked irritated -- he was cringing.</p>
<p>"Nick had a publicist go over to the DJ and demand he stop playing Mariah's tracks."</p>
